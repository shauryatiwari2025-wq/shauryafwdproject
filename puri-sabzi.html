Puri Sabzi

Prep Time: 30 mins Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 large Potatoes (cubed)
  • 2 cups Whole Wheat flour (Atta)
  • Cumin seeds, Turmeric, and Hing (Asafoetida)
  • Green chilies and Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Sauté spices and potatoes to make a spicy dry or semi-gravy sabzi.
  2. Knead a firm dough with wheat flour, salt, and water.
  3. Roll out small circles of dough.
  4. Fry the puris in smoking hot oil until they puff and turn golden.
  5. Serve hot with a side of pickle or yogurt.
