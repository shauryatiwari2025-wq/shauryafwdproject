Chole Bhature

Prep Time: 45 mins (plus soaking) Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • All-purpose flour (Maida) for Bhature
  • Onion, Tomato, and Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Chole Masala, Cumin, and Dried Pomegranate seeds
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook the chickpeas with tea bags for dark color.
  2. Sauté onions and spices to create a thick gravy base.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer until the gravy thickens.
  4. Knead the bhatura dough with yogurt and let it rest.
  5. Roll and deep fry the bhaturas until they puff up beautifully.
