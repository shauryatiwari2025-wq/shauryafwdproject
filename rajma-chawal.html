Rajma Chawal

Prep Time: 40 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Red Kidney Beans (Rajma)
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • Tomato puree and finely chopped onions
  • Turmeric, Red Chili powder, and Rajma Masala
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the soaked Rajma until soft and tender.
  2. Prepare a masala by frying onions, ginger-garlic, and tomatoes.
  3. Mix the beans with the masala and mash a few beans for thickness.
  4. Steam the Basmati rice with a leaf of bay and cloves.
  5. Serve hot Rajma over a bed of fluffy white rice.
