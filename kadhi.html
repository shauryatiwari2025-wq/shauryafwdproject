Kadhi Pakoda

Prep Time: 50 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sour Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 1/2 cup Gram Flour (Besan)
  • Fenugreek seeds (Methi) and Curry leaves
  • Sliced onions for Pakodas
  • Mustard seeds and dried Red Chilies for Tadka

Instructions

  1. Whisk yogurt and besan with water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Cook the batter on low flame, stirring constantly until thick.
  3. Prepare small onion fritters (pakodas) and deep fry them.
  4. Drop the pakodas into the simmering kadhi.
  5. Top with a hot tempering of oil, mustard seeds, and chili.
