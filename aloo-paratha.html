Aloo Paratha

Prep Time: 20 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 Boiled Potatoes
  • 2 cups Wheat Flour
  • Green Chilies & Ginger
  • Garam Masala & Salt
  • Ghee or Butter

Instructions

  1. Mash the potatoes and mix with spices.
  2. Roll out a small dough ball.
  3. Place the potato filling in the center and seal it.
  4. Roll it again gently into a flat circle.
  5. Cook on a tawa with ghee until golden brown.
